What Does a Novel Look Like From the Inside?

Interactive Visualizations — Computational Analysis of Cortazar's Rayuela

These interactive charts accompany a two-part article on using AI to map the hidden structure of Julio Cortazar's Rayuela (1963). Each chapter was measured at four independent scales — holistic embeddings, content-free stylometrics, LLM-perceived style, and explicit narrative DNA — then both reading paths were tested against 5,000 random orderings. All visualizations are built with Plotly: hover for details, drag to pan, scroll to zoom, click legend items to toggle layers. 3D charts can be orbited by dragging.
