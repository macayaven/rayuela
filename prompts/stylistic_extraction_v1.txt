DIMENSIONS AND RUBRIC:

3. punctuation_expressiveness

4. prose_rhythm

5. descriptive_density

   1 = Pure narration or monologue, no dialogue at all

7. code_switching_intensity

8. paragraph_density

9. narrative_distance

10. repetition_and_pattern

    10 = Heavy use of anaphora, parallelism, refrains, or obsessive repetition of motifs

11. typographic_experimentation
    1 = Completely standard formatting and layout
    10 = Unusual visual layout: missing punctuation, unconventional spacing, visual play with the page, stream-of-consciousness without conventional markers

12. syntactic_variety
    1 = Monotonous sentence structure; every sentence follows the same pattern
    10 = Wide range of sentence types: fragments, questions, exclamations, imperatives, long-short alternation, lists mixed with flowing prose
